Mission — उद्देश्य

← Home

Yogilib exists to preserve historical documents, treaties, records, manuscripts, letters and everything else pertaining to Nepal. This site is dedicated to the memory of Yogi Narharinath — for Nepali history and culture is incomplete without his work.

This site is run by an individual, not an institution. It is sponsored on a strict contribute whenever you want, how much you want basis.

This site would not be possible without Govinda Kafle. It is his mission that continues here.
